City air quality detector

We often tend to underestimate the importance of the quality of the air we breathe. We take it for granted. Well, guess what: it's not. Air quality is a very important factor for both the health of people and for that of ecosystems.

This app will help you figure out if you live or need to travel in a polluted city, so you can take appropriate precautions.
